Computational Domain

Standard neural networks excel at function approximation but lack an inherent understanding of physical boundaries. My research centers on enforcing conservation laws, symmetries, and boundary conditions directly within the loss function of deep learning systems.

By embedding partial differential equations (PDEs) directly into network architectures, we reduce data dependency and build predictive models that conform to the physical universe.
